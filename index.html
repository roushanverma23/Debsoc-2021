About Us

If you don’t go after what you want, you’ll never have it. If you don’t ask, the answer is always no. If you don’t step forward, you’re always in the same place.

The Debating Society gives you an opportunity to enhance and showcase your hidden talents and skills. Got creative writing skills? Or are you good at art and craft? Or do you know the titbits of website management. Debsoc has got it all covered for you. Keeping a positive mindset so you don’t let negative thinking get the best of you. Debsoc lets you explore opportunities based on your self interests with lots of chances to organise and structure events in the college that will help you prove your worth in the college.
